(02) — Profile

I build the whole thing — interface, backend, commerce, and the brand around it.

I'm Mahdi Ouatah, a full-stack developer who likes to own a product from the database to the last pixel of motion. Most days that means React, Next.js and TypeScript on the front, Node, Strapi and Supabase on the back, and Stripe wiring the commerce in between.

What I care about is the finish — the typography, the transitions, the way a checkout feels under your thumb. With Couleva I took it further and built an entire brand from zero: identity, headless CMS, a programmatic video engine, and the storefront that sells it.
